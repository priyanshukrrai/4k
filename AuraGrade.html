Capabilities

Studio‑grade tools, zero learning curve

🔍

Intelligent analysis

Reads resolution, duration, motion and average tone of your clip, then auto‑suggests the ideal Aura grade and upscale target.

🎨

Aura color grading

A GPU color pipeline emulating an After‑Effects colorist — lift/gamma/gain, teal‑orange split toning, vibrance and white balance.

🔬

Detail & denoise

Unsharp‑mask sharpening recovers crisp edges while adaptive denoise smooths grain — cleaner footage at higher resolution.

⬆️

Up to 4K upscale

Re‑renders every frame to your chosen resolution, all the way to 2160p UHD, with high‑quality resampling.

⚡

Live before / after

A real‑time WebGL preview with a draggable split so you can judge the grade frame‑accurately before exporting.

🔒

Private by design

100% client‑side. Your footage is processed on your own device and never uploaded to any server.
